Here we asked the question, "What percentage of the total population does each grade consume in an average highschool?" We found out that freshaman are aroud 22.7%, sophmores are around 27.7%, juniors are around 26.8%, and seniors are around 22.8%. We chose this type of graph, the pie chart, to easily display the way a highschool is broken up into different groups.

Data Source: https://centraldigest.com/news/2015/09/22/centrals-populations-increases-by-46-to-962-boys-outnumber-girls-by-60-students/
